Title: Finance Terms Glossary

EMI: Equated Monthly Installment, a fixed monthly loan repayment.
Interest: The cost of borrowing or the return on savings.
Inflation: The rise in general prices over time.
Liquidity: Ease of converting assets into cash.
Collateral: Asset pledged as loan security.
Budget: Plan for income and expenditure.
Credit Score: A number showing a person's creditworthiness.
Diversification: Spreading investments to reduce risk.
Mutual Fund: A pooled investment managed by professionals.
