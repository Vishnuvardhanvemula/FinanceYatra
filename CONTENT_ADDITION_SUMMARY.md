## 💡 Quick Start Guide for Adding New Content

### Using the Template

1. **Copy Template** from `contentTemplates.js`
2. **Fill in Metadata**: id, title, subtitle, duration
3. **Write Content** following the structure:
   - What is [Topic]?
   - Key Features (4-6 points)
   - Types/Categories
   - Examples & Calculations
   - Advantages & Disadvantages
   - Tips & Best Practices
   - Common Mistakes
4. **Add 5-6 Quiz Questions**:
   - 2-3 MCQs
   - 1-2 Scenarios
   - 1 Calculation (if applicable)
   - 1 True/False
5. **Write Key Points** (3-4 concise takeaways)
6. **Review** against quality checklist

### Time per Lesson
- Research: 10 minutes
- Content writing: 20 minutes
- Quiz creation: 15 minutes
- Review & formatting: 10 minutes
**Total: ~45-60 minutes per lesson**
