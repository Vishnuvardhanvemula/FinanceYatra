Title: Financial Literacy FAQ

Q: Why should I save money?
A: Savings protect you during emergencies and help achieve goals.

Q: What is a good credit score?
A: 750 or above is considered good in India.

Q: How can I avoid online fraud?
A: Never share OTP or banking details. Verify payment links and report fraud to your bank.

Q: What is the benefit of insurance?
A: It provides financial protection against risks like illness, accidents, or death.

Q: How do mutual funds work?
A: They collect money from many investors and invest in various securities to generate returns.
