## 📚 Recommended Official Sources for Verification

### For Indian Banking Information:

1. **Reserve Bank of India (RBI)**
   - Website: https://www.rbi.org.in
   - Use for: Banking regulations, interest rate trends, RBI guidelines

2. **Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation (DICGC)**
   - Website: https://www.dicgc.org.in
   - Use for: Deposit insurance limits and coverage details

3. **Income Tax Department**
   - Website: https://www.incometax.gov.in
   - Use for: Tax slabs, Section 80C limits, TDS rules, ITR filing

4. **Individual Bank Websites**
   - Check official websites of banks like SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, PNB, etc.
   - Use for: Current interest rates, fees, account features, minimum balance

5. **National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI)**
   - Website: https://www.npci.org.in
   - Use for: UPI guidelines, payment system information

6. **Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)**
   - Website: https://www.sebi.gov.in
   - Use for: Investment regulations, mutual funds, stocks

## 🎯 What You Should Verify:

- **Recurring Deposit**: Currently stated as 5% - 8% (verify with your bank)
- **Overdraft**: Currently stated as 10% - 14% (verify with your bank)

### Regulatory Limits (May Change):
- **DICGC Insurance**: ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank (check DICGC website)
- **Section 80C Limit**: ₹1.5 lakh per year (check Income Tax website)
- **TDS Threshold**: ₹40,000 for general, ₹50,000 for seniors (check IT website)
- **ATM Free Transactions**: 5 at own bank, 3-5 at others (check RBI website)

### Fees and Charges:
- **Demand Draft**: ₹50 - ₹100 (varies by bank)
- **Minimum Balance**: ₹1,000 - ₹10,000 for savings (varies by bank and account type)
- **ATM Charges**: ₹20-21 after free limit (verify with your bank)
- **Cheque Book**: May be free or charged (varies by bank)
